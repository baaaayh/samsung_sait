AI Researcher of the Year

  • Jason D. Lee

    He published several influential papers in theory of deep learning, reinforcement learning, optimization, LLMs and AI safety including NeurIPS best student paper award.

  • Emma Pierson

    She has made a number of society-shaking observations at the intersection of machine learning, society and healthcare. She has been doing interesting work on algorithmic fairness and ML for healthcare.

  • Xiang Ren

    He published many papers in the field of NLP including 3 best paper awards (ACL, NAACL, and WWW). He is also researching on LLMs, neuro-symbolic reasoning and knowledge graphs.

  • Connor W. Coley

    He has pushed the boundaries of molecular/material design using advanced AI technologies and also along the way made contributions to AI itself. He has received many outstanding prizes and still fairly early in career.

  • Virginia Smith

    She has made significant contributions to federated learning decentralizing data and training large scale models, as well as to a broader set of distributed and efficient learning and inference.
