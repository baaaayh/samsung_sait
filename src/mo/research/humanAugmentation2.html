The advent of revolutionary healthcare devices

Overview With the increase of life expectancy, the prevalence of chronic illness, and the rise of medical costs, there have been rapidly growing interests in mobile healthcare technologies. Mobile healthcare devices can help people to manage their healthy status by continuously monitoring users’ physical conditions. Many people today use mobile devices to track their steps, physical fitness, heartbeat and their sleeping patterns. Advanced healthcare sensors and healthcare solutions are key components in establishing these mobile healthcare devices as personal health managers.

Technology Trend Mobile healthcare devices continuously sense, analyze, and transmit various data including vital and non-vital signals. These data are acquired via wearable and/or implantable sensors regardless of place or time. This information enables healthcare specialists or artificial intelligence to monitor user’s conditions at any time, provide appropriate health advices in time, and assist users to keep proper physical activity levels. This is a truly revolutionary system to improve the quality of life.

SAIT Technology In react to rapidly evolving healthcare industry, SAIT is performing world-class research on mobile healthcare sensors and solutions for commercial use. We devote ourselves to invent novel core technologies that can enable monitoring of health conditions in non-invasive manner. One example of our recent efforts is cuff-less blood pressure trend tracking solution. The blood pressure monitoring solution is based on unique SAIT-developed algorithm and added to Galaxy phones and watches. This technology will help people manage their health status related to cardiovascular disease. The healthcare technologies SAIT has been developing will be eventually integrated into our daily lives via smart personal devices such as wearable devices, smart phones, tablet PCs, and smart TVs; providing a novel insight of the future healthcare service.
