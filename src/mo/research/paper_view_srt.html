Abstract
Main new contents (1) Maximum IR laser energy for Xe(5%)/Ne mixture is 20 mJ when the laser diameter is 60 um. (2) LTS (Laser Thomson Scattering) measurement was applied to the Xe (5%)/Ne PDP gas. (3) The electron temperature distribution is similar to that of Ne/Ar mixtures. (4) The relatively high electron density of this measurement is due to the measured position set to the high electron density peak in the striation.
