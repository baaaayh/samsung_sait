About SAIT

SAIT Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology is Samsung Group's R&D hub, established as the incubator for cutting-edge technologies under the founding philosophy of Boundless search for breakthroughs. And guided by the vision of Changing the World through Creative Research.

SAIT embraces
the follow missions
Missions
  • We develop the world's best or world's first technologies for new markets.
  • We create new convergence technologies.
  • We advance science-based nanotechnology research.
  • We develop disruptive technologies going beyond current paradigms.
